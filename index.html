mission & values

My name is Tomas, I’m a chef with 20 years of experience and a life-long love for good food. In the recent years, I’ve become really passionate about plant-based diet, seeing the enormous benefits it has for our health, our planet, for the animals. The last bit I was struggling with on this journey was to give up cheese. So I’ve began experimenting with creating my own dairy-free and cruelty-free cheese alternatives and perfecting the recipes over time until I was truly satisfied with taste and texture. “Cashew Tree” is my passion project and I’m happy to finally share my products with YOU. Our mission is to create delicious artisan vegan cheese that satisfies those cravings, while still doing good for the planet, animals and your body. We want to bring you cheese alternatives that are unprocessed, crafted by hand, using whole ingredients and are full of flavour. Our cheese can be enjoyed by all – vegan, lactose-intolerant people and non-vegans alike.

our products

Our cheese is healthy, ethical and sustainable. It’s made from cashew nuts, it’s great source of protein, good for your gut and can be enjoyed as a part of healthy diet. We use traditional cheese-ageing methods and bacterial culturing methods to make “Cashew Tree” vegan cheese. Every product is handcrafted in small batches. We offer multitude of flavourful varieties
